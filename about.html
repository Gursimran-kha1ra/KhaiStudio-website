About Preepix

Capturing love in stillness and motion

From a Spark to Storytelling

Preepix began from a simple spark — a deep passion for capturing moments that often go unnoticed. What started as curiosity quickly became a purpose: to turn real-life moments into memories that last forever.

I’m Pawan, a Vancouver-based photographer and the creative force behind Preepix. Every photo I take is driven by emotion, connection, and the desire to preserve stories in their most authentic form.

A Woman-Owned Photography Studio in Vancouver

Preepix is a woman-owned photography studio based in Metro Vancouver, built on passion, creativity, and a strong belief in storytelling through visuals.

Each session is approached with care, intention, and a focus on making clients feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

Photography With Meaning

Whether it’s a quiet portrait, a couple’s connection, or a lively event, the goal remains the same — to capture genuine moments and transform them into timeless images.

The focus is not just on how a photo looks, but how it feels years later when you look back at it.

My Approach to Photography

Photography is more than just images — it’s about emotion, energy, and authenticity. I believe the best photos come when you feel natural, not overly posed.

From the first conversation to the final delivery, my focus is on creating an experience that feels effortless while delivering results that feel meaningful.
