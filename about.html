Meet Pawan

From a Spark to Storytelling

Khai Studio began from a simple spark — a deep passion for capturing the moments that often go unnoticed. What started as curiosity quickly became a purpose: to turn real life into memories that last forever.

I'm Pawan, a Surrey-based photographer and the creative force behind Khai Studio. I serve couples, families, and storytellers across Metro Vancouver — Surrey, Langley, Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Coquitlam, and beyond. Every photograph I take is driven by emotion, connection, and the desire to preserve your story in its most authentic form.

A Personal Approach

Khai Studio is based in Surrey, BC, and proudly serves the entire Metro Vancouver region. Built on creativity, intention, and a strong belief in storytelling through visuals, the studio offers photography that feels personal — not generic, not rushed.

Each session is approached with care, attention, and a focus on making clients feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

Photography With Meaning

Whether it's a quiet couple session along the Fraser River, a wedding day full of ritual and family, or a destination elopement — the goal stays the same: capture genuine moments and transform them into timeless images.

The focus is not just on how a photo looks, but how it feels years later when you look back at it.

My Approach

Real, Natural, Timeless

Photography is more than images — it's about emotion, energy, and authenticity. I believe the best photos happen when you feel natural, not overly posed.

From the first conversation to the final delivery, my focus is on creating an experience that feels effortless while delivering results that feel meaningful. I'm happy to photograph anywhere — Surrey, White Rock, Langley, Burnaby, New Westminster, and well beyond.
